CloseExcessTabs!

Created by an annoyed IT person.

I took the time to add some options. You can either close tabs at random, close the first tab, or stop the new tab entirely until you decide to close it. You can also specify how many tabs you want to allow yourself open at one time. So stop complaining about your computer being slow when the problem is you can't be bothered to close out your tabs! Your IT department and computer friends will thank you when you learn that.
